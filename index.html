Hoarding Cleanup Process

What can i do about a hoarder? There are few institutions in Toronto that can help with a hoarder disorder but they normally wouldn't take care of the property, just the mental health condition, Toronto hoarding and disaster solutions specializes in cleaning a hoarder's location and their clutter and dealing with additional issues caused by it, like Mould, Pest, Hazardous Waste and etc.

Our approach to hoarding cleaning focuses on establishing mutual trust and respect with the individual and their representatives before any cleaning begins. We have the equipment and know-how to clean-up any case of hoarding no matter how small or extreme and we make sure that all affected persons are an integral part of the cleaning process.

Items that are to be kept are separated and organized away from items that are to be disposed. We will donate, recycle or dispose of all unwanted items in an environmentally friendly manner.
