Scrap Cars and Junk Vehicles Removal

At Toronto Hoarding and Disaster Solutions, we offer hassle-free junk car removal services. Here's our simple process:

  1. Contact Us: Give us a call or fill out our online form to schedule a pickup for your junk vehicle.
  2. Assessment: Our team will assess the condition of your vehicle and provide you with a fair quote.
  3. Pickup: Once the quote is accepted, we'll schedule a convenient time for you, to tow away your junk / scrap car.

Hoarding and Extreme Cleaning in Toronto, GTA

Toronto Hoarding and Disaster Solutions has been a trusted provider of professional hoarding cleaning, extreme cleaning, junk removal and disinfection services in Toronto for over 18 years. We specialize in assisting homeowners, landlords, and property managers in restoring their properties while complying with Ontario's Health and Fire Code.

Our team can be as discreet as possible, understanding the complexities of hoarding cases and working with individuals facing mental and physical disabilities. Over the years, we have collaborated with mental health service providers, lawyers, real state agents, social workers, property owners, and property management companies.

Our hoarding extreme cleaning process begins with a phone call and a preliminary quote. We then come to your location to further carefully remove all belongings, furniture, and accumulated garbage, adhering to strict industry protocols for discarding health hazardous contaminants such as needles, pet, and pest droppings.

We are well-versed in fire, health, and safety city codes, ensuring prompt resolution of all violations. we do have equipment to filter the air in the location and also we can remediate any mold or damage to the property. Our commercial steam cleaners effectively remove filth, dirt, stains, and disinfect surfaces simultaneously. Additionally, our machines producing dry vapor disinfect all surfaces they come in contact with, we do have trucks and trailers to provide transportation for the junk and hazardous materials to proper final destinations following with the strict Ontario Environmental Protection Code.
